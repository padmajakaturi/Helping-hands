About Helping Hands

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to making the world a better place. Our mission is to support communities by providing essential items such as food, clothing, books, and financial assistance. With your generous donations, we are able to reach people in need and make a difference.

Food Donations

We collect and distribute food to those who are struggling to meet their daily nutritional needs. Your donation can help provide meals to families in need.

Clothing Donations

We accept gently used clothing to help individuals and families who cannot afford new garments. Your clothing can keep someone warm and provide dignity.

Book Donations

Donate your unused books to help children and adults gain access to educational materials. Knowledge is power, and your books can change lives.

Monetary Donations

Financial contributions help us to fund our programs and purchase the necessary resources to support communities in need. Every donation counts.
